\vspace*{1cm}
\begin{abstract}
This document describes the activities carried out within Task 5.3 concerning the design and the hardware configuration of the robots that will be used in the project. It includes descriptions of the different devices that will be mounted on the robots, of the computational units, and of the connections and data communication among them.
\end{abstract}
